WHO AM I?

Mary Ara Ngembu

I began my academic journey with a BSc. in Applied Accounting from Oxford Brookes University, UK, building on my initial BSc. degree from the University of Buea, Cameroon. I furthered my professional qualifications by becoming a member of the Association of Certified Chartered Accountants (ACCA-UK) and the Certified Professional Accountants (CPA-Canada).

While working as an accountant, I spent countless hours reporting on past financial performance, which felt like a routine cycle. I began to question the true impact of my work and wanted to find ways to make organizations more proactive and efficient. Two questions drove my curiosity: How can we better predict future performance? And how can we streamline accounting tasks?

My quest for answers led me on a path to become a seasoned STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) professional. The turning point was when I stumbled upon a book titled 'Automate the Boring Stuff with Python' by Al Sweigart. The title alone convinced me that Python could unlock new possibilities in my field.

I immersed myself in learning, realizing that data analytics tools could revolutionize my role. Python was just the beginning. I found that data science offered the comprehensive skill set I needed to make a significant impact. Through platforms like Coursera, Udemy, and YouTube, I gained essential knowledge, earning the IBM Data Science Professional Certification along the way.

My first role as a data analyst confirmed my passion for data science. To further my expertise, I pursued a Master's Degree in Data Science at Eastern University, Pennsylvania, USA, graduating in 2022, with honors and a GPA of 3.65/4.

Currently, I work as a data scientist at Goodwill Industries of Alberta, where I lead data analytics and visualization projects to provide business insights. I also develop Python backend applications to automate processes such as file transfers.

I am deeply passionate about Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Applied Mathematics. My journey reflects a commitment to leveraging data to drive meaningful change in the technologically advancing business world.
